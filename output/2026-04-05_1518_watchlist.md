Movado's exceptional Quality and Value ranks are justified by genuine operational discipline and sensible portfolio management, not accounting tricks. However, the gap between Quality/Value ranks and the weak Momentum rank reflects real uncertainty: the company has stopped bleeding but has not yet convinced the market it can grow. For UK investors, the risk-reward is balanced rather than asymmetric—fair value, not a screaming buy.
